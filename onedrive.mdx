## How Deep Research Works in [Dume.ai](http://Dume.ai)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Question Intake & Planning">
    When you ask a complex question like _“Summarize our research insights on X and draft an executive summary,”_ Dume.ai uses multi‑step planning, breaking down research tasks just like Deep Research tools do.

    ![Screenshot 2025-07-13 at 2.24.48 AM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-07-13at2.24.48AM.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Semantic Retrieval via Vector Search">
    It ingests data from your integrated tools, embeds text content, and performs a vector search to extract relevant documents or emails using models like text‑embedding‑3 or bge‑small via Weaviate or pgvector

    ![Screenshot 2025-07-13 at 2.25.41 AM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-07-13at2.25.41AM.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Iterative Reasoning with Agent Chains">
    It ingests data from your different pages, embeds text content, and performs a vector search to extract relevant documents  using models like text‑embedding‑3 or bge‑small via Weaviate or pgvector.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Drafting & Reporting">
    Dume.ai compiles a structured summary or report based on your inputs and source, generating cohesive, cited findings in a readable format.
  </Step>
</Steps>
