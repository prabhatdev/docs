## **What you can do with it?**

You can ask [Dume.ai](http://Dume.ai) to create summarise or search for a google sheets. It can also create and new sheet file and save to it.

## How to integrate?

1. Login to [Dume.ai](http://Dume.ai)
2. Go to [Integrations](https://app.dume.ai/integrations) and select available integrations.
3. Click connect, you'll be redirected to Google Sheet and allow the permissions.
4. Once OAuth is complete, [Dume.ai](http://Dume.ai) can then access all your Google Docs.
5. You can tell Dume read, create, and search google sheets on your behalf.

## Example commands

- Go through the task allocation sheet and check who has worked on what.
- Go throught the monthly orders sheets and total the order amount.
- Fetch my spendigs from Paypal and create a table with date time amount and store in in google sheet.
