## **What you can do with it?**

As you can see in below example, you can easily fetch your emails and provide useful insights from it. Like this user is fetching all his food delivery bills and analysing monthly spends.

![Email Demo](/images/email_demo.png)

## How to integrate?

1. Login to [Dume.ai](http://Dume.ai)
2. Go to [Integrations](https://app.dume.ai/integrations) and select available integrations.
3. Click connect, you'll be redirected to Gmail and allow the permissions.
4. Once OAuth is complete, [Dume.ai](http://Dume.ai) can then access all your emails.
5. You can tell Dume to read, draft, and reply to emails on your behalf.

## Example commands

1. Check for all transaction alert emails I recevied last month and summarise my spending.
2. “Draft a reply to John's new email saying I can meet tomorrow at 9am.”
3. “Check for emails from Github and tell me all the PRs I need to reivew.”
