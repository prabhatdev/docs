## **What you can do with it?**

You can ask [Dume.ai](http://Dume.ai) to go through your PRD and create Jira tickets. Or get project progress report all from a simple prompt.

![Jira Demo](/images/jira_demo.png)

## How to integrate?

1. Login to [Dume.ai](http://Dume.ai)
2. Go to [Integrations](https://app.dume.ai/integrations) and select available integrations.
3. Click connect, you'll be redirected to Jira and allow the permissions.
4. Once OAuth is complete, [Dume.ai](http://Dume.ai) can then access all your jira projects.
5. You can tell Dume read, create, and update jira tickets on your behalf.

## Example commands

- Tell me high priority jira tickets assigned to me.
- Go through this PRD and create Jira Tickets based on it for Developers, Product and QA teams.
- Summarise the status of this jira project and tell me who's working on what.
