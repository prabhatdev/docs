## **What you can do with it?**

You can ask [Dume.ai](http://Dume.ai) to create PRD or search for a something specific or just get context about your projects, to help you better.

![Confluence Demo](/images/confluence_demo.png)

## How to integrate?

1. Login to [Dume.ai](http://Dume.ai)
2. Go to [Integrations](https://app.dume.ai/integrations) and select available integrations.
3. Click connect, you'll be redirected to Confluence and allow the permissions.
4. Once OAuth is complete, [Dume.ai](http://Dume.ai) can then access all your confluence pages.
5. You can tell Dume read, create, and search confluence pages on your behalf.

## Example commands

- Tell me recent confluence projects.
- Search for recent confluence projects created by me.
- Post a PRD template in confluence under project PROJ
