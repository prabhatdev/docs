## Setup

1. Select personalisation at down left corner menu and click on Auto Research
2. Enter you LinkdIN/Twitter or personal website URL.
3. Click Research Profile and let Dume go and visit your LinkdIn and find out more about your profession.
4. Once completed, Dume will learn everything about you and provide you tailored responses based on your whatever it learnt about you.
