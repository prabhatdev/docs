## Key Features

- **Simple Prompts:** Describe your desired image in plain language.
- **Fast Generation:** Get results quickly.
- **High-Quality Output:** Generate high-resolution images suitable for various applications.

## How to Use Dume.ai for Image Generation

1. **Enter Your Prompt:** Type a description of the image you want to create. Be as specific as possible to guide the AI.
2. **Generate:** Click the "Generate" button.
3. **Review and Refine:** Evaluate the generated image and refine your prompt if needed for better results.
